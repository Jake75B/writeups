Access control vestibules
- Opening one door causes others to lock (airlock)
- E.g. When one is open, the other cannot be unlocked
- Managed control through area to evaluate identity i.e. card reader + security check

Fencing
- Build a perimeter (very obvious)
- Prevent climbing, high fence/razor 

Video surveillance
- CCTV
- Motion detection
- object detection (face, number plate)

- Security guard
	- Physical protection at the reception area
- Two person integrity
	- Minimise exposure to attack, no single person has access to physical asset
- Access badge
	- Picture, name
	- electronically logged
	- integrated with electronic lock
- Lighting
	- Attackers avoid light
	- Specialised design
		- Good lighting angle for facial recognition
		- Overall light level
		- avoid shadow and glare
- Sensors
	- Infrared, common in motion detectors
	- Pressure, detect a change in force
	- Microwave, detect movement across a large areas
	- Ultasonic, receive reflected sound waves
